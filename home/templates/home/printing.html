POSTFLY our story

POSTFLY was founded in 2001 as an online printing company under the care of Grafische Groep Matthys. POSTFLY is there for all your combined printing, from flyers to posters, from business cards to brochures. We started with an office at the Keienbergweg in Amsterdam-Zuidoost with a team of five people. With our move to the Hoogoorddreef in Amsterdam-Zuidoost, we were able to make our work more and more efficient. There are now two members of the original team active and work is carried out from the head office in Turnhout, Belgium or completely corona-proof from home.

Grafische Groep Matthys is located in the graphic city of Turnhout in Belgium, it is run by 4 brothers, the third generation of this family business.

Together we have the expertise to guide all your orders from A to Z.
We have several recent printing presses, and we have all modern finishes in-house. We combine the printing forms so that we can offer them competitively, but also if you need specialist printing or striking packaging material you have come to the right place. Since 2008 we have specialised in the “inline cold foil” technique, which allows beautiful effects on your printed matter or packaging.

Feel free to take a look at our products page or request a quotation from us for different or specialist requirements!
