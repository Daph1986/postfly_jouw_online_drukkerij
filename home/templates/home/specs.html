It is import that you send your digital files according to our specifications, we ensure the correct processing of your file, but errors in the layout or in the digital files do not entitle you to a free reprint.

When formatting your files, you have to take a number of things into account. Check your design using the checklist below:

  • Resolution: 300 dpi
  • Color setting: CMYK, 300% maximum ink coverage and no overprint
  • Bleed: 3 mm all around, meaning on all sides
  • File type: PDF or JPG
